# CAM — Teacher Install Guide

**Who needs this app:** Only **teachers/admins** install CAM. Students do **not** use the app — they are only checked in/out by a teacher.

**Before you start:** You'll need the login (email + password) created for you by the center admin. If you don't have one yet, ask the admin to add your teacher account.

---

## 📱 If you have an Android phone

1. Open this link on your phone: **https://expo.dev/accounts/tengdeleons-team/projects/center-attendance-monitoring/builds/1df2936c-1280-462c-a3cb-8449e1f06d8a**
2. Tap **Download** / **Install**.
3. Android will warn about "installing from unknown sources" — tap **Settings → Allow from this source** (this is normal for apps not from the Play Store), then go back and tap **Install** again.
4. Open the **CAM** app from your home screen.
5. Log in with your teacher email + password.

*You only allow "unknown sources" once. After that, updates install normally.*

---

## 🍎 If you have an iPhone

The iPhone version isn't available yet. For now, please use an **Android phone or the center's shared Android device** to check people in/out. iPhone access is planned for a later update — the admin will let you know when it's ready.

---

## First time you log in

- Pick a person from the roster (search by name; filter teacher/student).
- Choose **Check In** or **Check Out**.
- The camera opens — take the person's **selfie** (this is required; no selfie, no record).
- The entry saves automatically with the date and time.

## Two normal things to expect

- **The first check-in each morning may take ~30 seconds.** The server "wakes up" after being idle overnight. This is normal — just wait for it. After the first one, it's fast.
- **No internet? Keep going.** If the network drops, CAM saves your check-ins on the phone and uploads them automatically when the connection returns. You'll see a small "pending" note until they sync.

## ⚠️ Before capturing any student's selfie

The center must have **signed parental/guardian consent** on file for that student (required by the Data Privacy Act, RA 10173). Do not check in a student whose consent form hasn't been collected. Ask the admin if you're unsure.
